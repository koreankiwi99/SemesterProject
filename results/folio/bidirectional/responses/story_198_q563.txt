Premises: When the Monkeypox virus occurs in a being, it may get Monkeypox. 
Monkeypox virus can occur in certain animals.
Humans are mammals.
Mammals are animals.
Symptoms of Monkeypox include fever, headache, muscle pains, and tiredness. 
People feel tired when they get the flu.
Conclusion: There is an animal.

-- Monkeypox virus can occur in certain animals.
axiom premise2 : ∃ b : Being, Animal b ∧ OccursMonkeypoxVirus b

-- Humans are mammals.
axiom premise3 : ∀ b : Being, Human b → Mammal b

-- Mammals are animals.
axiom premise4 : ∀ b : Being, Mammal b → Animal b

-- Symptoms of Monkeypox include fever, headache, muscle pains, and tiredness.

-- People feel tired when they get the flu.

-- Conclusion: There is an animal.
theorem goal : ∃ b : Being, Animal b := by
  obtain ⟨x, hx⟩ := premise2
  exact ⟨x, And.left hx⟩
