Premises: If a film is appropriate for people of all ages, then children can watch it without guidance from parents.
If a film contains excessive erotic and violent content, children cannot watch it without guidance from their parents.
If a film is rated General Audience, then it is appropriate for people of all ages.
All family-friendly animated films are rated General Audience.
All movies in the Frozen series are family-friendly animated films.
“Hachi: A dog’s Tale” is a film.
“Hachi: A dog’s Tale”  is either a family-friendly animated film or is appropriate for people of all ages. 
Conclusion: "Hachi: A dog's Tale" contains excessive erotic and violent content or is in the "Frozen" series or both.

PROVE TRUE ATTEMPT
======================================================================

axiom Film : Type
axiom Hachi : Film

axiom AppropriateAllAges : Film → Prop
axiom ChildrenCanWatchWithoutGuidance : Film → Prop
axiom ExcessiveEroticViolent : Film → Prop
axiom RatedGeneralAudience : Film → Prop
axiom FamilyFriendlyAnimated : Film → Prop
axiom FrozenSeries : Film → Prop

-- Premises
axiom premise1 : ∀ f : Film, AppropriateAllAges f → ChildrenCanWatchWithoutGuidance f
axiom premise2 : ∀ f : Film, ExcessiveEroticViolent f → ¬ ChildrenCanWatchWithoutGuidance f
axiom premise3 : ∀ f : Film, RatedGeneralAudience f → AppropriateAllAges f
axiom premise4 : ∀ f : Film, FamilyFriendlyAnimated f → RatedGeneralAudience f
axiom premise5 : ∀ f : Film, FrozenSeries f → FamilyFriendlyAnimated f
axiom premise6 : True  -- “Hachi: A dog’s Tale” is a film (modeled trivially)
axiom premise7 : FamilyFriendlyAnimated Hachi ∨ AppropriateAllAges Hachi

-- Conclusion to analyze:
-- "Hachi: A dog's Tale" contains excessive erotic and violent content or is in the "Frozen" series or both.
theorem conclusion : ExcessiveEroticViolent Hachi ∨ FrozenSeries Hachi := by
  -- From premise7, Hachi is either family-friendly animated or appropriate for all ages.
  -- In either case, it is appropriate for all ages, hence children can watch without guidance.
  have hApp : AppropriateAllAges Hachi :=
    Or.elim premise7
      (fun hffa => premise3 Hachi (premise4 Hachi hffa))
      (fun happ => happ)
  have hCan : ChildrenCanWatchWithoutGuidance Hachi := premise1 Hachi hApp
  -- Therefore, Hachi cannot contain excessive erotic and violent content.
  have hNotExcess : ¬ ExcessiveEroticViolent Hachi := by
    intro hEx
    have hNotCan : ¬ ChildrenCanWatchWithoutGuidance Hachi := premise2 Hachi hEx
    exact hNotCan hCan
  -- However, from ¬ExcessiveEroticViolent Hachi we cannot derive the desired disjunction
  -- ExcessiveEroticViolent Hachi ∨ FrozenSeries Hachi without additional information.
  sorry
