Premises: Everyone who can register to vote in the United States can participate in the 2024 United States presidential election.
If someone has United States citizenship, then they can register to vote in the United States.
A person either has United States citizenship or Taiwanese citizenship.
No Russian Federation officials hold Taiwanese citizenship. 
Vladimir neither holds Taiwanese citizenship nor is he a manager at Gazprom. 
Ekaterina she can register to vote in the United States, or she is a Russian federation official.
Conclusion: Ekaterina can participate in the 2024 United States presidential election or Vladimir is a manager at Gazprom.

axiom Participate2024 : Person → Prop

axiom TaiwanCitizen : Person → Prop
axiom RFOfficial : Person → Prop
axiom ManagerGazprom : Person → Prop

-- Premises
axiom prem1 : ∀ x : Person, CanRegisterUS x → Participate2024 x
axiom prem2 : ∀ x : Person, USCitizen x → CanRegisterUS x
axiom prem3 : ∀ x : Person, USCitizen x ∨ TaiwanCitizen x
axiom prem4 : ∀ x : Person, RFOfficial x → ¬ TaiwanCitizen x
axiom prem5 : ¬ TaiwanCitizen Vladimir ∧ ¬ ManagerGazprom Vladimir
axiom prem6 : CanRegisterUS Ekaterina ∨ RFOfficial Ekaterina

-- Conclusion
theorem conclusion : Participate2024 Ekaterina ∨ ManagerGazprom Vladimir := by
  have hPart : Participate2024 Ekaterina := by
    cases prem6 with
    | inl hReg =>
      exact prem1 Ekaterina hReg
    | inr hOff =>
      have hnotTw : ¬ TaiwanCitizen Ekaterina := prem4 Ekaterina hOff
      have hUSorTw : USCitizen Ekaterina ∨ TaiwanCitizen Ekaterina := prem3 Ekaterina
      have hUS : USCitizen Ekaterina := by
        cases hUSorTw with
        | inl hUSC => exact hUSC
        | inr hTw => exact False.elim (hnotTw hTw)
      have hReg : CanRegisterUS Ekaterina := prem2 Ekaterina hUS
      exact prem1 Ekaterina hReg
  exact Or.inl hPart
