Premises: True or not true is true. "Stranger Things" is a popular Netflix show.
If a Netflix show is popular, Karen will binge-watch it.
If and only if Karen binge-watches a Netflix show, she will download it.
Karen does not download "Black Mirror."
"Black Mirror" is a Netflix show.
If Karen binge-watches a Netflix show, she will share it with Lisa.
Conclusion: Karen will share "Stranger Things" with Lisa.

axiom download_implies_binge : ∀ s : TVShow, OnNetflix s → Download s → Binge s

axiom binge_implies_share : ∀ s : TVShow, OnNetflix s → Binge s → ShareWithLisa s

-- Conclusion: Karen will share "Stranger Things" with Lisa.

  exact binge_implies_share StrangerThings ST_netflix hB
