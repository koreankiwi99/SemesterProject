Premises: True or true is true. "Stranger Things" is a popular Netflix show.
If a Netflix show is popular, Karen will binge-watch it.
If and only if Karen binge-watches a Netflix show, she will download it.
Karen does not download "Black Mirror."
"Black Mirror" is a Netflix show.
If Karen binge-watches a Netflix show, she will share it with Lisa.
Conclusion: "Black Mirror" is popular.

-- If Karen binge-watches a Netflix show, she will share it with Lisa.

-- From the premises we can actually derive that "Black Mirror" is NOT popular.
theorem not_popular_BlackMirror : ¬ Popular BlackMirror := by
  have hN : Netflix BlackMirror := BlackMirror_is_Netflix
  have hiff : Binge BlackMirror ↔ Download BlackMirror := binge_iff_download BlackMirror hN
  -- From ¬Download BM and (Binge BM ↔ Download BM), we get ¬Binge BM

  -- Therefore, ¬Popular BM

  exact h_not_binge (h_imp hPop)
