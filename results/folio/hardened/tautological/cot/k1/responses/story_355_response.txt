Premises: False or not false is true. All people who eat salads regularly are very conscious about their health and eating habits.

All people who disregard their physical well-being are not very conscious about their health and eating habits.
If people visit the gym at least once a day, then they always fulfill their daily nutritional intakes.

- If someone disregards their physical well-being (D), then they are not very health-conscious: D -> ¬C.
