Premises: If Kevin submits the financial report to the board, then the board reviews the financial report. If the board reviews the financial report, then the budget is approved for the next quarter. The finance team knows that either Kevin submits the financial report or the team conducts a detailed audit, or both. If the finance team conducts a detailed audit, then the audit discovers discrepancies. If the IT department updates the accounting software, then the accuracy of financial projections improves. The team also believes that either they conduct a detailed audit or the accuracy of financial projections does not improve, or both. If the audit discovers discrepancies, then the financial strategy is revised with accurate data.
Conclusion: If the budget was not approved for the next quarter and the IT department updated the accounting software, was the financial strategy revised with accurate data?
