Premises: Everyone who embraces new ideas either creates innovative work or inspires others. Kenna either encourages free expression or follows conventional rules, but not both. If someone creates innovative work, then they think outside the box and foster creative growth. Kenna either appreciates conformity or values individuality, but not both. Kenna does not appreciate conformity. Kenna either follows conventional rules or thinks outside the box, but not both. Kenna either embraces new ideas or collaborates with others. If someone values individuality, they may not always foster creative growth, and vice versa. If Kenna collaborates with others or welcomes diverse perspectives (or both), then she creates innovative work. Kenna encourages free expression. If someone values order and prefers structure, then they follow conventional rules.
Conclusion: Kenna does not welcome diverse perspectives.

-- Conclusion: Kenna does not welcome diverse perspectives.

  have hVI : ValuesIndividuality Kenna := by
    cases premise4 with
    | inl h =>
      have : False := premise5 h.left
      exact this.elim
    | inr h =>
      exact h.left
  -- From premise8, ValuesIndividuality → ¬FostersCreativeGrowth

    (And.left (premise8 Kenna)) hVI
  -- From premise9 and premise3, (Collaborates ∨ Welcomes) → Fosters
  have hCWtoFoster : (CollaboratesWithOthers Kenna ∨ WelcomesDiversePerspectives Kenna) → FostersCreativeGrowth Kenna := by

    have hCreate : CreatesInnovativeWork Kenna := premise9 h
    have hTF := premise3 Kenna hCreate
    exact hTF.right

  intro hWelcome
  have hCW : CollaboratesWithOthers Kenna ∨ WelcomesDiversePerspectives Kenna := Or.inr hWelcome
  have hFoster : FostersCreativeGrowth Kenna := hCWtoFoster hCW
  exact hNotFoster hFoster
