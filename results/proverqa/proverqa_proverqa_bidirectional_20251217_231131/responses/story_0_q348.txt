Premises: If Saul finds an inspirational figure, then he will either become a success or pay tribute to that figure, but not both. Saul does not continue to create. Saul practices diligently. If someone gains recognition and develops a unique voice, then they will continue to create. If someone explores their own style, they may not necessarily find an inspirational figure, and vice versa. Saul draws from influences. Saul values feedback. If Saul produces original work, then he will earn critical acclaim. Saul does not earn critical acclaim. If Saul either stays true to himself or values feedback (but not both), then he will remain authentic. If Saul diligently practices and stays true to himself, then he will gain recognition. Saul does not remain authentic. If Saul either draws from influences or pays tribute to a figure, then he produces original work. If someone explores their own style, then they will develop a unique voice.
Conclusion: Saul does not become a success.

  FindsIF Saul →
    ((BecomesSuccess Saul ∧ ¬ PaysTribute Saul) ∨ (PaysTribute Saul ∧ ¬ BecomesSuccess Saul))

axiom premise2 : ¬ ContinuesToCreate Saul
axiom premise3 : PracticesDiligently Saul
axiom premise4 : ∀ x, (GainsRecognition x ∧ DevelopsUniqueVoice x) → ContinuesToCreate x

-- The ambiguous "may not necessarily" premise is omitted due to its non-deductive nature.

axiom premise6 : DrawsFromInfluences Saul
axiom premise7 : ValuesFeedback Saul
axiom premise8 : ProducesOriginalWork Saul → EarnsCriticalAcclaim Saul
axiom premise9 : ¬ EarnsCriticalAcclaim Saul
axiom premise10 :
  (((StaysTrueToSelf Saul ∧ ¬ ValuesFeedback Saul) ∨ (¬ StaysTrueToSelf Saul ∧ ValuesFeedback Saul)) → RemainsAuthentic Saul)
axiom premise11 : (PracticesDiligently Saul ∧ StaysTrueToSelf Saul) → GainsRecognition Saul
axiom premise12 : ¬ RemainsAuthentic Saul
axiom premise13 : (DrawsFromInfluences Saul ∨ PaysTribute Saul) → ProducesOriginalWork Saul
axiom premise14 : ∀ x, ExploresOwnStyle x → DevelopsUniqueVoice x

-- Conclusion: Saul does not become a success.
theorem conclusion : ¬ BecomesSuccess Saul := by
  intro hSuccess
  -- From draws-from-influences, he produces original work
  have hProd : ProducesOriginalWork Saul := by
    exact premise13 (Or.inl premise6)
  -- Then he earns critical acclaim
  have hEarn : EarnsCriticalAcclaim Saul := premise8 hProd
  -- But this contradicts the premise that he does not earn critical acclaim
  exact premise9 hEarn
