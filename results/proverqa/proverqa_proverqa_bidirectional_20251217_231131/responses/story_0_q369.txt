Premises: Yareli is respectful of boundaries. Yareli leads medical teams. Yareli does not build trust with patients. If Dr. Yareli Hernandez is empathetic, then she is either respectful of boundaries or listens actively (but not both). If Dr. Yareli Hernandez leads medical teams, then she either communicates effectively or builds trust with patients. If Dr. Yareli Hernandez is a dedicated doctor, then she gains international recognition and improves patients' lives. Any person who listens actively or cares for others can build strong relationships. Dr. Yareli Hernandez is empathetic. Dr. Yareli Hernandez cares for others or puts patients first. Anyone who puts their patients first is a dedicated doctor. Yareli does not build strong relationships.
Conclusion: Dr. Yareli Hernandez does not gain international recognition.

axiom p3 : ¬ BuildsTrustWithPatients Yareli

  -- From p7 and p11, derive ¬(ListensActively Yareli ∨ CaresForOthers Yareli)
  have hNotLAorCares : ¬ (ListensActively Yareli ∨ CaresForOthers Yareli) := by
    intro hOr
    apply p11
