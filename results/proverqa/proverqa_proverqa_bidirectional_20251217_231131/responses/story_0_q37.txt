Premises: Buster does not respond to calls. All dogs that listen to commands and behave properly are well-trained. Buster is either obedient or playful, but not necessarily both. Buster follows routines. Buster is either playful or loving (or both). If Buster either responds to calls or follows routines (but not both), then he listens to commands. All well-trained dogs are obedient and have good manners. Buster behaves properly.
Conclusion: Buster is loving.
