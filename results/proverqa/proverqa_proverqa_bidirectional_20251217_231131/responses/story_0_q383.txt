Premises: If someone listens to their heart, then they will find inner peace. If Mariah has creative talent, then she can either achieve fame or find inner peace, but not both. Mariah enjoys solo travel. Mariah trusts her intuition. If Mariah values education, then she has creative talent and expresses emotions. Anyone who trusts their intuition will listen to their heart. If Mariah enjoys solo travel, then she is either open-minded or values her privacy, but not both. Everyone appreciates knowledge or values education. Mariah does not appreciate knowledge. Mariah values her privacy.
Conclusion: Mariah achieves fame.

axiom ax2 : CreativeTalent Mariah → ((AchievesFame Mariah ∨ InnerPeace Mariah) ∧ ¬(AchievesFame Mariah ∧ InnerPeace Mariah))
axiom ax3 : EnjoysSoloTravel Mariah
axiom ax4 : TrustsIntuition Mariah
axiom ax5 : ValuesEducation Mariah → (CreativeTalent Mariah ∧ ExpressesEmotions Mariah)
axiom ax6 : ∀ x : Person, TrustsIntuition x → ListensHeart x
axiom ax7 : EnjoysSoloTravel Mariah → ((OpenMinded Mariah ∨ ValuesPrivacy Mariah) ∧ ¬(OpenMinded Mariah ∧ ValuesPrivacy Mariah))
axiom ax8 : ∀ x : Person, AppreciatesKnowledge x ∨ ValuesEducation x
axiom ax9 : ¬ AppreciatesKnowledge Mariah
axiom ax10 : ValuesPrivacy Mariah

-- From the premises we can derive that Mariah does not achieve fame.
theorem not_fame : ¬ AchievesFame Mariah := by
  -- From "everyone appreciates knowledge or values education" and "Mariah does not appreciate knowledge",
  -- deduce that Mariah values education.
  have hVE : ValuesEducation Mariah := by
    have h := ax8 Mariah
    cases h with
    | inl hAK => exact False.elim (ax9 hAK)
    | inr hVE => exact hVE
  -- From valuing education, Mariah has creative talent.
  have hCT_Em := ax5 hVE
  have hCT : CreativeTalent Mariah := And.left hCT_Em
  -- From creative talent, we get "fame or inner peace" and "not both".
  have hxor := ax2 hCT
  have hNotBoth : ¬ (AchievesFame Mariah ∧ InnerPeace Mariah) := And.right hxor
  -- From trusting intuition, Mariah listens to her heart, hence finds inner peace.
  have hListens : ListensHeart Mariah := ax6 Mariah ax4
  have hIP : InnerPeace Mariah := ax1 Mariah hListens
  -- If she had fame, together with inner peace we'd contradict "not both".
  intro hFame
  exact hNotBoth (And.intro hFame hIP)

-- Requested conclusion (not derivable from the premises)
theorem goal : AchievesFame Mariah := by
  -- This cannot be proven from the given premises; in fact, we have shown ¬ AchievesFame Mariah above.
  sorry
