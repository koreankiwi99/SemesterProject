The statement "Some orchestras premiered music pieces" can be proven true based on the given context. Specifically:

1. Vienna Music Society is an orchestra (given).
2. Symphony No. 9 is a music piece (given).
3. Vienna Music Society premiered Symphony No. 9 (given).

This directly provides an example of an orchestra (Vienna Music Society) that premiered a music piece (Symphony No. 9).

<lean>
axiom obj : Type
axiom SymphonyNo9 : obj
axiom ViennaMusicSociety : obj
axiom MusicPiece : obj → Prop
axiom Orchestra : obj → Prop
axiom Premiered : obj → obj → Prop  -- Premiered music orchestra

axiom ax1 : MusicPiece SymphonyNo9
axiom ax2 : Orchestra ViennaMusicSociety
axiom ax3 : Premiered SymphonyNo9 ViennaMusicSociety

theorem exists_orchestra_premiered : ∃ (o : obj) (m : obj), Orchestra o ∧ MusicPiece m ∧ Premiered m o :=
  by
    apply Exists.intro ViennaMusicSociety
    apply Exists.intro SymphonyNo9
    exact ⟨ax2, ax1, ax3⟩
</lean>

ANSWER: True
